2.BARBELL-BENCH-PRESS

The barbell bench press is a popular compound exercise for building upper body strength, primarily targeting the chest, shoulders, and triceps. It is a fundamental lift in the sport of powerlifting and a staple in bodybuilding and general fitness routines[5 x 2]

3.INCLINE-DUMBBELL-PRESS

The incline dumbbell press is a strength exercise that primarily targets the upper portion of the chest muscles (the clavicular head of the pectoralis major).[5 x 2]

4.DIPS

Dips are a popular compound exercise used to build strength in the upper body, primarily targeting the chest, shoulders, and triceps. They can be performed on parallel bars, a power tower, or an assisted dip machine.[5x2]

5.DUMBBELL-FLYES

A dumbbell fly (or flye) is an isolation exercise that primarily targets the chest muscles. Unlike a pressing movement, a fly keeps a constant angle in the elbows and relies on a sweeping, arc-like motion of the arms to engage the pectoral muscles.[5 x 2]

6.PUSH-UPS

A push-up is a fundamental bodyweight exercise that builds strength in the chest, shoulders, triceps, and core. As a compound exercise, it works multiple muscle groups at once and is a versatile part of any fitness routine because it requires no equipment.[10 min]
